# Video Generation Prompts & Storyboard: FaktriIQ Product Promo

This document contains visual storyboards and prompt sequences designed for advanced video generation models (such as Sora, Runway Gen-3 Alpha, or Kling AI) to create a premium, photorealistic product video for **FaktriIQ**. 

The style is strictly **real-life commercial cinematography**—it must look like an authentic, high-end corporate advertisement shot on professional film gear, with absolutely no cartoonish, CGI, synthetic, or AI-style artifacts.

---

### 🎨 Visual & Cinematic Style Guidelines
* **Camera & Optics**: Shot on Arri Alexa LF, Cooke Anamorphic lenses, 35mm format, realistic lens flare, natural optical defocus, shallow depth of field. Subtle, organic film grain.
* **Lighting**: Natural, volumetric, high-contrast industrial lighting. Rays of sunlight slicing through windows with realistic dust motes. No artificial neon glows.
* **CGI & AI Prevention**: Real-world textures and imperfections. Human actors must have natural skin pores, realistic hair, fabric fibers, and clothing folds. Industrial equipment must show raw steel surfaces with realistic scuffs, smudges, and direct glass glare. No glossy 3D renders, no Unreal Engine style, and no smooth synthetic textures.
* **Color Palette**: Volt & Carbon theme. Slate grays, natural industrial warnings, crisp whites, and metallic finishes.

---

### 🎬 Scene-by-Scene Prompt Sequence

#### 🎥 Scene 1: The Field Technician (Mobile Copilot)
* **Visual**: A photorealistic close-up of an industrial chemical plant technician (40s, weathered face, detailed skin pores) wearing a high-vis safety jacket and a yellow hardhat with scuffs. He stands in front of a massive, complex network of real stainless-steel chemical pipes. He is holding a rugged smartphone.
* **Motion**: Slow, steady camera slide (dolly track) focusing from the technician's eyes down to the screen, showing the app interface.
* **Prompt**:
  > Photorealistic cinematic close-up of a 40-year-old chemical plant technician with realistic skin details, wearing a yellow hardhat and safety glasses. He holds a rugged mobile phone showing the 'FaktriIQ' Q&A interface. Shot on Arri Alexa 35mm, Cooke Anamorphic lens, natural volumetric sunlight slicing through a factory window in the background, shallow depth of field. Realistic, tactile textures of steel pipes, fabric folds, and weathered skin. No CGI, no cartoon, 8k.

#### 🎥 Scene 2: On-Device Q&A (Tactile Interaction)
* **Visual**: Close-up of a gloved hand (heavy-duty work glove, realistic fabric weave, dirt smudges) typing on a phone screen. The screen shows direct light reflections. Text appears on screen: "nitrogen purging protocols". The interface looks like a real mobile app, returning a structured list of safety steps.
* **Motion**: Handheld camera movement, slight natural jitter.
* **Prompt**:
  > Close-up shot of a dirty, heavy-duty industrial work glove typing on a rugged smartphone screen. The screen shows natural glare and smudges, displaying the text 'nitrogen purging protocols' and loading compliance checklist cards. Authentic handheld camera motion, organic film grain, shallow depth of field. Real textures, no clean CGI, no synthetic UI lines, looks like a real mobile screen capture.

#### 🎥 Scene 3: The EHS Officer Workstation (Compliance Audit)
* **Visual**: A clean, quiet, and realistic safety coordinator's office inside a manufacturing plant. A female officer (30s) sits at her desk, lit naturally by window light. Her dual-monitor workstation displays the FaktriIQ desktop two-pane compliance dashboard comparing plant SOPs with regulatory text.
* **Motion**: A slow, flat tracking shot from left to right across the desk.
* **Prompt**:
  > Cinematic slow pan across a realistic industrial EHS coordinator's office. A female safety officer sits at a desk with two large monitors displaying compliance checklists with subtle red and green text indicators. Natural, soft daylight from a side window, realistic desk clutter, paper documents, and office textures. Shot on Arri Alexa LF, 35mm film look, authentic workplace, no glossy CGI or rendering styles.

#### 🎥 Scene 4: Corporate Outro & Brand Slogan
* **Visual**: A clean, solid-matte white wall in an industrial design studio. The minimalist geometric logo "FaktriIQ" appears in matte charcoal text, with the slogan below: "Catch compliance gaps before your next audit does."
* **Motion**: The camera slowly dollys forward toward the wall as the text is revealed.
* **Prompt**:

A clean, matte-painted white wall with the logo 'FaktriIQ' in solid dark slate, and the slogan 'Catch compliance gaps before your next audit does' written underneath in clean sans-serif typography. Soft, natural shadow casting across the wall, authentic architectural shot, 35mm film grain, 8k.
